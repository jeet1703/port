About Twinkle Sharma

Twinkle Sharma is a renowned Classical Dance Teacher from India, empowering students through the art of Kathak and Bharatanatyam. Starting her dance journey at the age of five, Twinkle's early passion for the arts was nurtured under the loving guidance of her maternal grandmother. Her talent blossomed as she grew, performing on prestigious stages and eventually dancing in front of Russian President Vladimir Putin by the age of eighteen.

However, like many women, Twinkle faced societal pressures that led her to temporarily pause her career to fulfill family obligations. Rather than allowing her passion to fade, she reignited her artistic aspirations and founded the Shri Natraj Nritya Academy, where she now mentors and trains the next generation of classical dancers.

Twinkle’s story is one of resilience and dedication, reminding us all that it’s possible to balance personal responsibilities with professional ambitions. Her journey stands as a powerful example to women everywhere, encouraging them to continue striving for their dreams no matter the circumstances.

With multiple accolades under her belt, including the prestigious Nritya Guruvarya Award and the Top 100 Women Icon Award, Twinkle continues to inspire through her dance academies, teaching the classical art of Kathak and Bharatanatyam while challenging societal norms.

Through her work, Twinkle hopes to not only preserve the rich traditions of Indian classical dance but also to empower young women to pursue their passions and live their fullest lives.
